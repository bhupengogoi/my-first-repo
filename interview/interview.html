Following are the list of common Interview Questions

  1. Introduce Yourself ?
  2. Why do you want to work here ?
  3. What are your strengths and Weakness ?
  4. Why should we hire you ?
  5. What are your short and long-term goals ?
  6. How well do you handle stress and pressure ?
  7. What do you know about our company ?
  8. What's your greatest achievement till date ?
  9. What are your hobbies and interests ?
  10. What are the most important qualities in a team ?
  11. What type of environment do you prefer to work in ?
  12. How would you handle a disagreement with someone in authority ?
  13. If you experienced conflict with a Co-Worker. What would you do ?
  14. How would you deal with a complaint from a customer or a client ?
  15. Tell me a time when you made a mistake ?
  16. What motivates you ?
  17. What are your salary expectations ?
  18. Do you have any questions for us ?
